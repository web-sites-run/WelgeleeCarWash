Welgeleë Car Wash & Valet

About Us

Conveniently located in the underground parking area of the Wellington Welgeleë Shopping Center, our car wash offers professional, high-quality service with a personal touch. Our experienced staff and long-standing manager are dedicated to making your vehicle shine, with attention to detail and care you can trust.
Special equipment:

  • Foam gun for even distribution of high quality soap.
  • 360° sprayer to wash between the cab and canopy of your bakkie.
  • Industrial type compressors.
  • Industrial type pressure hoses.
  • Industrial type vacuums.
